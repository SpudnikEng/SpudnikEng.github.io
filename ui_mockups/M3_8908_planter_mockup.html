INFORMATION
Manual mode

- Operates the planter based on ground speed and specified seed spacing, without adjusting seed size.

Seed adjust mode

- Operates the planter based on ground speed and specified seed spacing, with adjusting seed size based on seed count sensors

Weight adjust mode

- Operates the planter based on ground speed and target lbs/acre, adjusting seed weight according to the weight lost from seed hopper Note: Since seed size impacts this mode, calibration is required for optimal accuracy

Rapid cal

- In Seed Adjust Mode, a seed size calibration will be automatically performed within 200 feet of planting

- In Weight Adjust Mode, a seed size calibration will be automatically performed within the first 200 feet of planting, followed by a seed weight calibration after 1,000 feet.

Auto rapid cal

- Found in Row Ctrl Settings Page will automatically force rapid Cal when outside seed size or seed weight range bounds.
